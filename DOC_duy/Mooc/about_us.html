Who is Florist Store?

Florist is a collaboration between co-founders Hannah Spilva and Verity Tuck. The idea of Florist started with a purpose rather than a product. They want to build a business that helps stay connected with loved ones and spread a little joy in the world. Han and Vez are living away from home, miss friends and family, and see a gap in the market for sending affordable, authentic, fun and thoughtful gifts and flowers.
