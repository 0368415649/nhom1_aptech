ABOUT US
LVLY is a collaboration between Co-Founders Hannah Spilva and Verity Tuck. The idea for LVLY started with a purpose rather than a product. They wanted to build a business that would help keep loved ones connected and spread a bit of joy in the world. Han and Vez were living away from home, missing friends and family and saw a gap in the market to send affordable flowers and gifts that were authentic, fun and thoughtful. Online Store.
